Solve real world challenges with the latest technology

Zeus Controls has been providing end to end cost-efficient, optimum industrial automation control solutions through innovative design and use of latest technology. We are a well-known brand in the Process Control and Instrumentation manufacturing industry offering a wide range of process control instruments like PID controllers, Smart Datalogging systems, RPM and Speed indicators, Ratio Indicators, Master-Slave clocks, PLC's, HMI's etc.


We undertake complex automation projects and provide complete end to end integration including hardware, networking and software - Both Local and cloud based IOT solutions.
